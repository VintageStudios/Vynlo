Get Started with Vynlo

This brief step by step guide shows how to get started with Vynlo. Follow the steps below to get started with Vynlo quickly.

  1. Click the "Continue" button below. This will open the authentication modal. (e.g.. the login/signup window)
  2. Either log in or create a new account.
  3. Once logged in, you can start adding media and creating playlists. There is a more formal guide available in the documentation.
